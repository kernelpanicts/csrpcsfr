About CSFR

The CSFR or "Carolina State Fire and Rescue" is a Sub-Department of Carolina State Roleplay with upwards of 100 people in service and handles the Fire and Medical calls within the servers. CSFR operates all over the state; in the city, country, mountains, and even in the water. They handle Fires, Hazmat Situations, Mountain Rescues, Water Rescues, and much more. Here is some information you should know:

  • • Weekly 5 hour requirement
  • • After submitting an application, do not DM anyone to ask about application status unless it has been 48 hours since submitting
  • • If accepted, join the CSFR Discord and go to the trainee-information channel. Once there, scroll up and read the top message
