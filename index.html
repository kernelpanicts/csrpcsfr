About CSFR

The CSFR or "Carolina State Fire and Rescue" is a statewide fire department with upwards of 130 members who serve the community by supporting Law Enforcement Officers, carrying out all types of rescues, suppressing and preventing fires, and much more. The department offers a unique form of roleplay outside of LEO and Civilian RP, Subdivisions for Water Rescue, and a community you will rarely find anywhere else. You will be responsible for the safety, care, and recovery of officers, civilians, and their property. Do you think you have what it takes to be a firefighter?
