Applicant Requirements

Before you apply, please note there are requirements you must meet. If you cannot currently meet these requirements, please wait until you can to apply. You must:

  • Be 14 years or older
  • Be able to put 5 hours a week in the department
  • Be able to stay focussed when neccessary and be a team player at all times
  • Be fluent in English as this is an English RP community

If you meet these requirements, you are ready to apply. Scroll down to the Link when you feel ready.

Department Preferences

The CSFR are looking for people who are willing to step out of the traditional "Cops and Robbers" area of play, and want to provide and partake in unique scenarios accross all servers. We are looking for people who can use their better judgement to find the best plan of action to complete rescues, put out fires, and generally assist their community. If you want to save people with medical issues, recover people who are stranded on land and at sea, or just want to support your community, that is what we do in the Carolina State Fire and Rescue.
